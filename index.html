About Me

Hello everyone,I'm KANAV ARORA, a first year B.tech(CSE) student of University of Petroleum and Energy studies, Dehradun. I completed my schooling from O.S.D.A.V. Public School from Kaithal district of Haryana.

It’s been 18 years that I have come to this planet. I grew up in a well-off family, and my parents are both teachers. One thing my mom told me when I was a little boy is that “Hey son! With your big head, you have a brain twice as big as the others. So you have to be the number one.” But these seem to be unfulfilling. I feel a sense of lack of meaning and fulfillment. The more I grow up, the more I see things differently and the more I want to change things in this world.

My thought of changing the world and my interest in Computer Science had brought me to this path and i look forward to engage myself completely in this field with full enthusiasm and curiosity.

I have always been an enthusiastic kid,sometimes an ambivert too. I love exploring new places and meeting new people and listening to their stories.I like adventures too . I have a firm belief in Steve Jobs's thought “The people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world are the ones who do.” And yes I belong to that group of crazy people.
